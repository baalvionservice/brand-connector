## Overview

**Baalvion Connect** is where brands and partners meet the Baalvion ecosystem — a platform for
collaboration, partnership onboarding, and engagement across the group's businesses.

## ✨ Features

- **Partner onboarding** — bring brands and collaborators into the ecosystem
- **Collaboration** — connect partners with the right Baalvion businesses
- **Engagement** — manage relationships and opportunities in one place
- **Responsive & fast** — optimized for every device
